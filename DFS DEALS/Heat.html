Heat Press

What Is Heat Press

Heat Press Printing makes use of your graphic design, which is transfered onto a special paper, called heat transfer paper which is coated in wax. This paper is then transfered into the machine and then onto your garment. This form of printing provides a sleek and smooth finish and is sure to leave you satisfied.

What Can Be Heat Pressed

  • Bages
  • Wood
  • Ceramics
  • Spandex
  • Cotton
  • Linen
  • Nylon
  • Polyester
  • Coasters
