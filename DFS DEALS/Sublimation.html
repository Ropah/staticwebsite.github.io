Sublimation

What Is Sublimation

Sublimation Printing is a chemical process, that turns the ink into a gas which enables the gas to penatrate the item. Your desired design is transfered onto a special paper which is used to penetrate the fibers of the material and create a permanant body, which helps to ensure that your design will be long lasting.

What Can Be Sublimated

  • Rigid Substrates
  • Signage
  • Polyster Fabrics
  • Ceramics
  • Photograph Printing
  • Linen
  • Knit Wear
  • Magnets
  • Apparel
