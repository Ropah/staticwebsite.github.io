DIRECT TO GARMENT

What Is DTG

Direct To Garment or DTG is a process of using aqueous ink jet technology, to apply a desired design on an item. The garment is pre-treated with a pre-treatment machine. The ink is then jetted or sprayed onto the textile. Due this being a digital process of printing the image or logo is therefore shaper and is higher in resolution.

What We Can DTG Print

  • Natural fibers, such as:
  • Bamboo
  • Hemp
  • Outwear
  • Cotton
  • Linen
